Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all browser extensions developed and published by Abdulaziz Alghamdi ("we", "our", or "us"), including DateConv, TabSaver, and any future extensions under the ASG brand.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Our extensions operate entirely on your local device.

Permissions Used

  • tabs — Used only to read the URLs and titles of your open tabs for the purpose of saving sessions locally. This data never leaves your device.
  • storage — Used to save your sessions and preferences locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API.
  • windows — Used to open a new browser window when restoring a saved session.

Advertising

Our extensions display a small advertisement banner loaded from a hosted page. This advertisement is served by EthicalAds, which does not use cookies or track personal user data. You can read EthicalAds' privacy policy at ethicalads.io/privacy-policy.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts to load fonts in our extensions. Google Fonts may log basic request data such as IP address. We do not use any other third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

Data Storage

Any data saved by our extensions (such as tab sessions) is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage. This data is never transmitted to any server and is only accessible by the extension on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
